We Are Using Kinds Of Artificial Intelligence To Understand Data And To Create Meaningful Solutions In The World Of Communication

Computer Vision


With the help of computer vision, people with hearing loss can have better access to employment opportunities, public announcements, and services, thus reducing the communication gap that exists between them and people with normal hearing.

Language Models


The system works by using computer vision to recognize hand gestures and movements used in sign language, which are then translated into spoken language or text. The system can also recognize spoken language and convert it into sign language for people with hearing loss
